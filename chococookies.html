Chocolate Chip Cookies

The chocolate chip cookies we make are exactly the recipe on the back of the Toll House chocolate chip bag! We have found that this is a tried and true recipe to go by.

The ingredients you'll need are:

  • 2 1/4 cups flour 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups of the Nestle Toll House chocolate chips
  • if you want you can add 1 cup of chopped nuts, though we've never done this
We did substitute M&Ms for the chocolate chips and love it! I'm itching to try PB M&Ms but have not yet.

To make:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 and prepare an ungraded baking sheet.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a larger mixing bowl (all the dough will end up here) beat the sugars, butter and vanilla extract until creamy.
  4. Add the eggs, one at a time, and beat each one until thoroughly mixed and creamy.
  5. Gradually add in the flour mixture -- beat thoroughly before adding additional flour mixture.
  6. Stir in choco chips and nuts.
  7. Drop onto baking sheet with a spoon. Bake 9-11 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
  8. Remember to let cool on either drying rack or wax paper for at least 3 minutes.
We have loved these chocolate chip cookies! The butter makes them nice and soft. Yum!
